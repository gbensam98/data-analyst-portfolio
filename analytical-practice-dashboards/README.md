## Student Performance Dashboard
**Domain:** Education  
**Objective:** Evaluate student performance across subjects and demographics to support academic decision-making.  
**Key Insights:**
-Students studying an average of 20 hours with high attendance (80%) tend to perform better.

Grade A students have the highest average exam scores, followed by B and C.

Positive peer influence and high teacher quality are linked to better exam performance.

Students with internet access consistently score higher than those without.

Tutoring improves performance, but excessive sessions do not always increase scores.

Students living closer to school perform better than those farther away.

Parental education level has a noticeable impact on student outcomes.
**Tools:** Microsoft Excel, Power Query, Pivot table
